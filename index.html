Biography

I am a Ph.D Student in Inria Lyon under the supervision of Rémi Gribonval and Elisa Riccietti, in the OCKHAM team led by Rémi Gribonval, working on the quantization and optimization of neural networks, studying their invariance by rescaling.

My Ph.D is financed by the project SHARP of the PEPR-IA.
